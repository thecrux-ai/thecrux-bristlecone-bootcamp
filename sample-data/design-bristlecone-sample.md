# design.md - Bristlecone brand (ready-made fallback)

Use this only if you could not pull a design.md from your own Bristlecone file in Part A.
Copy it into your `day2builds` folder, rename it to `design.md`, and the rest of the session works exactly the same.

These rules were pulled from a real Bristlecone delivery deck. Colours and fonts are the real brand. No client data is in here, just the look.

---

## Colours

Each colour has a role name. The build prompts in Session 5 use these role names, so keep them.

| Role name | Hex | Where it is used |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Brand Navy | `#192123` | The darkest colour. Report and deck titles, section headings, the title-slide background. |
| Brand Blue | `#28ACB3` | The Bristlecone teal. The main accent: header rules, chart primary colour, buttons, links. (Named "Brand Blue" so the build prompts find it.) |
| Sky Blue | `#C0E6F5` | The lighter second accent. Secondary chart series, soft highlights, card top borders. |
| Light Grey | `#CCE1E8` | Page and table backgrounds, section bands. A pale blue-grey, not a flat grey. |
| Mid Grey | `#7F7F7F` | Captions, footnotes, secondary text. |
| Ink | `#333536` | Body text. |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Text on dark backgrounds, card surfaces. |
| Status Green | `#00B050` | RAG = Green. On track. |
| Status Amber | `#FFC000` | RAG = Amber. Watch. |
| Alert Red | `#FF0000` | RAG = Red. At risk. Also for effort overruns and anything that must jump out. |

---

## Fonts

- **Headings:** Arial Black, falling back to Arial Bold, then a system sans-serif.
- **Body:** Calibri, falling back to Arial, then a system sans-serif.
- **Code or figures:** a monospace font (Consolas or similar).

Keep headings tight and bold. Keep body text regular weight, comfortable line spacing.

---

## Logo and wordmark

- The Bristlecone wordmark sits **top-left** of every report header and on the deck footer.
- On dark (Brand Navy) backgrounds, use the **white** version of the wordmark.
- Do not stretch it. Leave clear space around it.

---

## Report layout

- **Header:** Bristlecone wordmark top-left, report title and today's date top-right, a thin **Brand Blue** rule under the header.
- **Section headings:** **Brand Navy**, bold.
- **Tables:** **Light Grey** header row, white body rows, thin **Brand Blue** cell borders. Right-align numbers.
- **Status:** colour the RAG value with Status Green / Status Amber / Alert Red.
- **Footer:** "Bristlecone | Confidential" on the left, page number on the right.

---

## Proposal / SOW layout

- **Cover page:** Brand Navy background, white title, a **Brand Blue** accent bar, wordmark top-left.
- **Section headings:** Brand Navy with a **Brand Blue** underline.
- **Tables:** Light Grey header row, white rows.
- **Footer:** wordmark and page number.

---

## Deck layout

- **Title slide:** Brand Navy background, white title, a **Brand Blue** accent bar, the date bottom-left in **Sky Blue**.
- **Content slides:** white background, **Brand Navy** title, a thin **Brand Blue** underline under the title, footer with the wordmark and slide number.
- **Charts:** **Brand Blue** as the primary series, **Sky Blue** as the second series.
- **Status:** Status Green / Status Amber / Alert Red, never invented colours.
- **Emphasis:** use Alert Red sparingly, only for at-risk items.
